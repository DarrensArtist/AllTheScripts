Who we are we

At All The Scripts, we are passionate about empowering game developers and creators with the tools and resources they need to bring their projects to life. Whether you're a hobbyist, an indie developer, or just getting started, we're here to help you turn your ideas into reality.

What We Do

We provide a wide range of resources to streamline your game development process, including: Game Scripts and Simple Game Builds that can be easily recreated. Game Design Documents and Financial Spreadsheet Templates to keep your project organized. High-quality 3D Models, Music, and other assets to bring your game to life.

How We Operate

Our resources are mostly free, supported by the community through donations and small advertisements. Some premium resources are available to help cover the costs of running the site, but our focus remains on accessibility for all developers.

The Blog

Our blog is where you'll find detailed guides and insights on how to best use our resources. We offer tips, tutorials, and discussions to help you navigate the world of game development.
